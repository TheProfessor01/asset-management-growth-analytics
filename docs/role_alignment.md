# Role Alignment: Personal Investor Distribution Analytics

## Target Role Themes

This project is built to demonstrate readiness for a data analytics role supporting Personal Investor Distribution leadership. The role emphasizes Tableau, SQL, Python, executive-ready visual storytelling, business partnership, and advanced analytics.

## Portfolio Narrative

I built a SQL-first analytics case study that simulates a Personal Investor Distribution environment. The project shows how analytics can help leaders understand which lead sources produce high-quality clients, which advisors are most productive, how client books are growing, which client segments are at risk, and whether compensation incentives align with business outcomes.

The work includes a synthetic distribution dataset, a relational data model, SQL transformations, KPI views, validation checks, Tableau-ready reporting outputs, and planned Python-based advanced analytics. The goal is to demonstrate the ability to move from business ambiguity to an executive-ready analytics product.
