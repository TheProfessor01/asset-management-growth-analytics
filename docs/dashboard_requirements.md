# Dashboard Requirements

## Primary Audience

The dashboard suite is designed for Distribution leadership, sales managers, advisor team leads, marketing partners, compensation strategy partners, and BI stakeholders.

## Design Principles

- Lead with decision-making, not raw activity
- Keep executive views concise and trend-oriented
- Provide drill-down paths for managers and analysts
- Separate production metrics from quality metrics
- Pair productivity measures with business value measures
- Make risks, outliers, and opportunities visually obvious

## Dashboard Suite

### 1. Executive Distribution Scorecard

Purpose: Provide senior leadership with a monthly view of distribution performance.

Core KPIs:

- New leads
- Contact rate
- Lead-to-client conversion rate
- New funded clients
- Net new assets
- Client retention rate
- Asset retention rate
- At-risk client count
- Advisor productivity index
- Compensation efficiency

Recommended views:

- KPI tiles with month-over-month change
- 12-month trends for conversion, net new assets, and retention
- Regional or team performance comparison
- Top opportunities and risks summary

### 2. Lead Source Quality Dashboard

Purpose: Help leaders decide which lead sources deserve investment.

Core KPIs:

- Lead volume by source
- Contact rate by source
- Conversion rate by source
- Funded client rate by source
- Average initial assets by source
- Net new assets from converted clients

Recommended views:

- Source quality ranking
- Conversion funnel by source
- Initial and follow-on assets by source
- Lead source mix over time

### 3. Advisor Productivity Dashboard

Purpose: Identify high-performing advisors, coaching opportunities, and capacity constraints.

Core KPIs:

- Leads assigned
- Calls completed
- Meetings held
- Follow-ups completed
- Opportunities closed
- New clients acquired
- New assets gathered
- Conversion rate
- Assets per converted lead

Recommended views:

- Advisor ranking table
- Activity-to-outcome scatterplot
- Productivity trend by advisor
- Team and region comparison

### 4. Client Segment and Retention Dashboard

Purpose: Surface under-engaged segments and clients with elevated retention risk.

Core KPIs:

- Clients by segment
- AUM by segment
- Engagement rate
- Days since last contact
- Asset decline rate
- Client retention rate
- At-risk client count

Recommended views:

- Segment growth and retention matrix
- At-risk client list
- Engagement distribution by segment
- Asset movement by segment

### 5. Compensation Alignment Dashboard

Purpose: Evaluate whether incentive payouts align with productivity and business value.

Core KPIs:

- Total incentive payout
- Payout per converted client
- Payout per dollar of net new assets
- Productivity rank
- Compensation rank
- Growth contribution share
- Payout share

Recommended views:

- Compensation versus productivity scatterplot
- Payout efficiency ranking
- Rank variance table
- Contribution share versus payout share comparison

## Tableau Build Expectations

- Use consistent colors for positive, neutral, and negative performance
- Avoid cluttered legends and unnecessary decoration
- Prioritize readable labels, tooltips, and filter behavior
- Provide executive summaries at the top of each dashboard
- Use drill-downs to support manager-level diagnosis
- Make dashboard actions support the business workflow
